Our Team

Barangay Chairman Antonio H. Santos Jr

This seasoned politician is an Engineer by profession. An unopposed Barangay Captain for two consecutive elections, he first served as Kabataan Barangay Chairman and Barangay Kagawad. With his wisdom and leadership quality, he has steered the Barangay to its finest stature.

Kagawad Wilmar T. Baluyot

This second-termer Barangay Kagawad is an entepreneur who owns various cellphone repair stalls. As an official, he was delegated either as Chairman or co-Chairman of various committees of the Barangay. At present, he is the Chair of the Public Order and Safety Committee.

Kagawad Felicisima C. Santos

During the entirety of her term as Barangay Kagawad, she has held on to the position as Chairperson of the Health and Medical Services Committee of the Barangay. Given this task, she magnificently performed her mandated duty with outstanding results.

Kagawad Romualdo G. Sta. Maria

This second-termer Barangay Kagawad instigated the installation of steel gates within the territorial jurisdiction of the Barangay. With regard to his mandated duties, he accomplishes them exquisitely. Presently, he's the Vice-Chair of the Public Order and Safety Committee.

Kagawad Ibrahim G. Tahir

At present, he holds the position of Chairperson of the Ecological Solid Waste Management Committee, this second-termer Barangay Kagawad was assigned to maintain the spick-and-span stature of the Barangay for a more better ambiance.

Kagawad Erwin C. Valdivia

A Co-Chair of the Youth and Sports Development Commitee, he's one of the factors for the success in the implementition of their committee's projects. He likewise served as Chairperson of the Public Order and Safety Committee. He's on his last term as Barangay Kagawad.

Kagawad Jocelyn M. de Leon

A newbie in the field of politics and as co-Chair of the Ecological Solid Waste Management Committee, she has earned the respect of her peers by performing her task of continously declogging every waterways in the barangay for a flood-free barangay.

Kagawad Teresita P. Ng

A last-termer Kagawad who has served her term fully by being co-chair of some of the barangay's committees. At present, she is the co-chairperson of the Health and Medical Services wherein she provides the monitoring of the sugar levels of the community.

SK Chairman Glen G. Ganit

As Chairman of the Youth and Sports Development Committee, he has steered the Youths growth thru sports and education, by properly implementing their proposed projects. He initiated the acquiring of desktop computers to be used for the blended learning program.

Secretary Jonathan A. Martinez

A former Barangay Kagawad who served the community alongside Barangay Captain Antonio Santos Jr. In the course of his term, he was the Vice-Chair of the Health and Medical Committee and the Peace and Order Committee. A CompSci student and an employee of Alilam Law Office.

Treasurer Modesta M. Janolino

Appointed as Barangay Treasurer during the term of former Barangay Chairman Stanly Ganitano, she continously weaves her expertise in this field by maintaing the Fiscal Budgets of the barangay, this time under the tutelage of Barangay Captain Antonio Santos Jr.

Ex-O Ariel M. Escorpiso

As Chief of the Barangay Police Security Officer (BPSO), he was tasked to maintain the peace and security of the community and prevent crimes and other underground activities from occuring at all times.

BHW Julieta E. Evangelista

This appointed barangay official was given the task to oversee the well-being and other medical status of the community. She likewise assists the Health and Medical Services Committee in the implementation of their programs. She is also the Assistant Chapel Coordinator of the barangay.
